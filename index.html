Questions answered

Common questions
we get asked.

If you have a question that isn't here, just ask us directly — we're always happy to talk.

How long is a typical engagement?

It depends on where you are. Foundation work is usually 6–8 weeks. Scaling work runs 3–6 months. We agree on outcomes upfront — when we hit them, we can wrap or continue.

Do you work with specific industries?

We've worked across real estate, raw materials, retail, hospitality, wellness, and more. The principles of growth are similar; the execution adapts to your market.

Can you work with an existing team?

Yes. We work best with founders, teams, and organisations ready to be hands-on partners. We're not external consultants — we embed and work alongside your people.

What if we don't have baseline metrics?

We build them. One of our first outputs is clarity on what we're measuring. You can't manage what you don't measure — so we start there.

What if the engagement isn't working?

We address it immediately. If there's no fit, we'll be direct about it. Our reputation is built on results, not delivered hours.

Can you work remotely or do I need you on-site?

Both. We're flexible on location — what matters is that we're integrated into your workflows. Most work is remote with strategic in-person sessions.
